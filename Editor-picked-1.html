Don’t let Instagram fashion influencers fool you!

Prarthana Vyavahare

October 22, 2023


No doubt, fashion has become a huge part of everyone’s life. It has been decades now, but the trends of fashion are still ongoing, nobody can ever get bored of fashion style and trends. Fashion is incredibly important to emerge the personality of a person. But everything has two sides “pros & cons” and it applies to fashion as well.

There are many mistakes people do while styling & here is a list of fashion mistakes you should avoid –

  • Inappropriate Accessories



    Sometimes less is really more. Over accessorizing doesn’t look good on anybody. Accessories are great but when you wear them all at once, it can get overwhelming. It is also very much important to wear the right accessory to the right occasion. Always make sure you choose a correct accessory to complete your outfit.

    This vintage jewellery store is the first of its kind in India. Celebrities from Deepika Padukone, Sonam Kapoor Ahuja, Kajol and Kiara Advani are regular patrons of the store.

  • Wearing The Wrong Size Clothes



    Do not wear clothes of the wrong size, it is essential to wear what fits your body. Wrong size clothes can make you look tacky. And it is not appropriate to wear clothes that does not flatter your figure. Being more involved in fashion, what you wear and how you wear is important.

  • Large Bags



    Bags play an essential role in every woman’s life. They create a statement of their own, but do not purchase a oversized bags, otherwise these bags will take attention away from you and it might seem that the bag is carrying you. The type of bag you choose has the power to make you or break your look.

  • Oversized Sunglasses



    Sunglasses can complete the absolute look of a person. But the fame your sunglasses must be in proportion to your face size. Oversized sunglasses might look extremely odd and trashy. Choose sunglasses that feel comfortable, these sunglasses will overwhelm your face.

  • Overlaying Of Powder



    Do not over do the powder on your face while makeup. It’s a translucent powder but it doesn’t mean it’s invisible. Apply a small amount of powder to set your base and do not make your skin look trashy. If you cover the makeup with powder it can look more heavy.

  • Mismatched Colors



    Choose the balance color for your outfit. Color does play an important role in our life. Mismatched Color would give you the worst look. Color makes the difference since it makes life not only meaningful but also beautiful. So choose your color wisely.No matter what patterns you’re wearing, the key is to understand the style.

  • Too Many Coats Of Mascara



    Do not apply too many coats of mascara to your lashes. This can make your lashes look too heavy. Two coats on each lash line is fine, overcoating is where you will mess up your eyeslashes and it can turn your whole look worst. The more coats you apply, the more likely you will get a dull look.

  • Big Bold Prints



    Big bold prints will definitely take away attention from you. Small prints would define your body shape and keep the whole look balanced and stylish. Big bold prints can be a bit distracting.
